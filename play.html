Chess Racer Plus

































Instructions: As an elite strategist you must work your way up the rank by capturing the other teams pieces. You may only capture pieces your rank or below.

By capturing 3 of your own rank you may progress to the next rank. Avoid higher ranks and your own team at all cost! Capture the other teams king and

Become The King!



Ranks: Pawn --> Knight --> Bishop --> Rook --> Queen --> King!

Controls:

Pawn| Left 1 Space: A | Right 1 Space: D

Knight| Left 2 Spaces: A | Right 2 Spaces: D

Bishop| Auto Move Left: A | Auto Move Right: D

Rook| Left 1 Space: A | Right 1 Space: D | Left 3 Spaces: Q | Right 3 Spaces: E

Queen| Auto Move Left: A | Auto Move Right: D | Left 3 Spaces: Q | Right 3 Spaces: E
