Instructions: You must work your way up the rank by capturing the other teams pieces. A piece may capture any piece it's rank or below. However only pieces it's own rank count twards ranking up! Capture 3 of your own rank to progress while avoiding higher ranks and your own team. Capture enough and become the king!

Ranks: Pawn --> Knight --> Bishop --> Rook --> Queen --> King!

Controls:

Pawn: Left 1 Space: A Right 1 Space: D

Knight: Left 2 Spaces: A Right 2 Spaces: D

Bishop: Auto Move Left: A Auto Move Right: D

Rook: Left 1 Space: A Right 1 Space: D Left 3 Spaces: Q Right 3 Spaces: E

Queen: Left 1 Space: A Right 1 Space: D Move to Left Edge: Q Move to Right Edge: E
